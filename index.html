About The Temple


Shri Pahadi Balaji Temple is amongst the most ancient temples in Udaipur. The Temple is situated on a mountain at the sides of Goverdhan Sagar Lake. The Temple is very magnificent . The Mount is also surrounded by gardens and greenery.

Miracles of this Temple


Owing to the remarkable miracles of Pahadi Balaji, people travel from far and wide to have a glimpse and receive his grace.
