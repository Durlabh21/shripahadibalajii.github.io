STHAPNA DIWAS


On 26th January every year, the Sthapna Diwas of Balaji is celebrated.


HOLI


Every year on the auspicious festival of Holi , the Holika Dahan is done and the next day Dhulandi is played and enjoyed by everyone.

HANUMAN JAYANTI


Every year the occasion of Hanuman Janmostav is celebrated with great warmth and devotion, where more than 10000 people come across the city to worship Lord Hanuman. On this day , in afternoon Sundarkand Paath is organised, followed by a function of Chappan Bhog , where the devotees bring Bhog to the Temple. In the evening after the MahaAarti, Mahaprasadi is organised by the Samiti Members and a Bhajan Sandhya is also organised which lasts till midnight. At 12 in the midnight the celebration comes to an end by Aarti .


KRISHNA JANMASHTAMI


Every year, Krishna Janmashtami is celebrated where functions like Bhajan Sandhya and Dahi Handi are organised and at midnight the birthday of Lord Krishna is celebrated by Aarti and Bhajans.


SOCIAL WORK


AYURVEDIC KADA VITRAN:

Kada , the traditional Indian Ayurvedic and medicinal drink , helps to boost our immunity. The Kada Vitran Programme is arranged by the members of the Samiti, where Kada is being distributed to people.


VRIKSHAROPAN:

Tree plantation is very important as trees provide us oxygen which is very necessary for our livelihood as well as, very important for our environment. With this thought, Vriksharopan is been organised by the Samiti Members and people takes part in it actively and works together to Save our Environment.
